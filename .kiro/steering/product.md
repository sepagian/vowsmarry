# Product Overview

VowsMarry is a comprehensive wedding planner SaaS platform designed for couples and wedding planners. The platform provides tools to manage all aspects of wedding planning including paperwork, budgeting, tasks, vendors, dresscode, rundown, and savings tracking.

## Core Features
- **Wedding Planner Modules**: Paperwork tracking, budgeting, to-do lists, vendor management, dresscode planning, event rundown, savings tracking, dowry management, and souvenir planning
- **Digital Invitations Add-on**: Template-based invitations, guest management, RSVP system, photo galleries, digital gifts, and love story sections

## Target Audience
- Primary: Engaged couples (25-45, tech-savvy) planning their own wedding
- Secondary: Wedding planners and vendors
- Tertiary: Wedding guests (via invitations and RSVP)

## Business Model
Freemium SaaS with premium templates, custom domains, vendor marketplace, and advanced analytics as monetization strategies.
